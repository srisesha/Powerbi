Discount Stratergy

Embedded PowerBi Environment



Details


Problem statement: The client wanted to get feedback from his department heads and executives about the discount performances on various products around the world. He wanted to add data continuously to the data sources which should update from time to time. He also should be able to get new insights simultaneously with the added data and wanted to have the track of who added the data and when?

This project includes extracting data from powerbi(which is data source here) and integrating it with powerapps. Power Apps are used to tackle the data easily and also it helps to update the data directly into powerBi. By adding triggers of Power automate we can even get the notification or mail on what changes are made by other members of the organization.

So this powerapps consist of 3 screens where first screen data is integrated with powerBi, second screen data is integrated with microsoft lists. Third screen is a simple success message that the update is added.This app has the list of all the products in the first screen followed by feedback inputs and editable data columns in the second one and a success message in the third screen. This app is used to view details of certain products and give feedback on the performances of discounts strategy of those products and make the decisions for further business plans
