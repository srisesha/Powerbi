World Finance Report

Embedded PowerBi Environment



Details


Problem statement: When the client wants to know which element of the organization is actually impacting the business growth and wants to check the codependencies between these elements. For example, which part of the world has the highest business value? Which part of the month has its highest compatibility with sales?Do profits have a raise by introducing discounts?

This project includes its 5 paged dash boards addressing each and every parameter that impacts the growth of the business. Parameters considered in this project are product names, segments, discounts, profits, selling price, time stamp, sales and locations.

First page explains the finances insights based on the location , second page explains the impact of time on Finances. The third page states the impact of discount and discount categories on various countries and over all growth in sales and profits. Forth and Firth pages focus specifically on sales and profits.

User Friendly: I have included filters in every page at the top. So that the user can check the specific parameters, they wanna get the insights on. Also one can check the changes taking place in the whole dashboard by clicking on one chart i.e it impacts all other graphs in a specific page and gives the insights on how one specific element impacts the remaining all elements.
